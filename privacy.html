Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 7, 2026

Review status: release-owner privacy review pending. This page is a release-preparation draft and must be checked against the shipped app and App Store Connect App Privacy answers before submission.

Overview

DoseDay is a local-first personal medication log for dose dates, user-entered symptoms, injection sites, reminders, notes, weight entries, and visit-summary exports. This policy explains the current data handling design for the iOS app.

Information You Enter

  • Medication names and dose amounts
  • Dose dates and timing
  • Injection sites
  • User-entered symptoms and severity
  • Weight entries
  • Notes and appointment questions
  • Reminder preferences

Local Storage

DoseDay is designed to store tracking history on your device using iOS local storage. The current app does not require an account and does not operate a DoseDay server for your health history.

Exports and Sharing

DoseDay lets you export visit summaries, CSV history, appointment notes, and data bundles through the iOS share sheet. When you share or export information to another app, person, or service, their privacy practices apply.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, DoseDay may schedule local notifications on your device. Notification permissions are controlled by iOS settings.

Purchases

DoseDay Pro is planned to use Apple StoreKit subscriptions. Apple may process purchase and subscription information under Apple's terms and privacy policies. DoseDay does not receive full payment card numbers from Apple.

Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking

The current app includes no advertising SDKs, no third-party analytics SDKs, and no third-party tracking. It does not include account login, CloudKit/iCloud sync, HealthKit, location services, third-party crash reporting, or a DoseDay server for health history. If analytics, crash reporting, cloud sync, accounts, support tooling, or third-party SDKs are added later, this policy and App Privacy disclosures must be updated before release.

Health and Medical Boundary

DoseDay is a personal log, reminder, and export tool only. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, emergency guidance, symptom interpretation, medication sourcing, or dose recommendations. Always follow your healthcare provider's instructions and seek urgent care for severe symptoms.

Data Retention and Deletion

Because DoseDay stores tracking history locally in the current app, deleting entries in the app or deleting the app from your device may remove local app data, subject to iOS behavior and any backups or exports you control.

Children

DoseDay is not designed for children. If age rating, account features, or child-directed features change, this policy must be reviewed and updated before release.
