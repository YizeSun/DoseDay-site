DoseDay Support

DoseDay is a personal medication log, reminder, and export tool. This page is the public support entry point for the app.

Before Public Release

Email support at martin.yize.sun@gmail.com. The release owner should verify that the published support path does not create a DoseDay health-history server or collect exported records unless the Privacy Policy and App Privacy answers are updated first.

What DoseDay Does

  • Records user-entered medication routine information.
  • Stores app data locally on device in the current build.
  • Supports reminders through iOS local notifications.
  • Exports visit summaries, CSV history, and local data bundles through the iOS share sheet.

What DoseDay Does Not Do

  • It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, emergency guidance, symptom interpretation, medication sourcing, or dose recommendations.
  • It does not replace instructions from a qualified healthcare professional.
  • It does not operate a DoseDay server for your health history in the current build.
  • It includes no advertising SDKs, no third-party analytics SDKs, and no third-party tracking in the current build.

For Medical Questions

Follow your healthcare provider's instructions. For severe symptoms or emergencies, contact emergency services or a qualified healthcare provider.
